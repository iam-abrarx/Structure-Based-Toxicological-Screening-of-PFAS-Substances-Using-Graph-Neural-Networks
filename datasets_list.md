# Project Dataset Inventory

This document lists all data sources utilized in the PFAS Risk Intelligence Platform.

## 1. Primary Structural Compendiums

Core lists used to define the chemical space of interest.

* **EPA PFAS Master List (Version 2)**
  * **File:** `data/raw/EPA PFAS Master List V2.xlsx`
  * **Source:** US Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)
  * **Content:** Authoritative list of Per- and Polyfluoroalkyl Substances, including CASRN, Names, and DTXSIDs. Used as the backbone for the "Global Risk Landscape".

* **PFOA/PFOS Supplementary Data**
  * **File:** `data/raw/tx0c00264_si_003.xlsx`
  * **Source:** Scientific Literature (Supplementary Info from Toxicology Study)
  * **Content:** Likely contains high-quality curative toxicity data or physical-chemical properties for key PFAS congeners.

## 2. Toxicology Data (Tox21 Program)

High-throughput screening data used to train the predictive models (Approach 5). These were scraped directly from PubChem/NCBI.

* **Source:** PubChem BioAssay Database (Tox21 Program)
* **Location:** `approach5_tox21/data_scraping/`
* **Endpoints Covered (approx. 69 assay files):**
  * **NR-PPAR-gamma:** Nuclear Receptor Peroxisome Proliferator-Activated Receptor Gamma (Metabolic disruption).
  * **SR-p53:** Cellular tumor antigen p53 (Genotoxicity/Stress).
  * **SR-HSE:** Heat Shock Element (Cellular Stress).
  * **SR-MMP:** Mitochondrial Membrane Potential (Mitochondrial toxicity).
  * **NR-AR:** Androgen Receptor (Endocrine disruption).
  * **NR-ER:** Estrogen Receptor (Endocrine disruption).

## 3. Environmental Monitoring Data

Real-world occurrence data used for "Real World Risk" integration.

* **Drinking Water**
  * **Files:**
    * `data/raw/Drinking water PFAS/TW_PFAS_Table_2a_9660_Results_v2.txt`
    * `data/raw/Drinking water PFAS/TW_PFAS_Table_2b_9662_Results_v2.txt`
  * **Content:** Detected levels of specific PFAS in treated drinking water samples.

* **Groundwater**
  * **Location:** `data/raw/GroundWater/`
  * **Content:** Spatio-temporal records of PFAS contamination in groundwater aquifers.

## 4. Computed / Derived Datasets

Data generated by the pipeline itself during execution.

* **Risk Matrices:** `final_risk_integration/final_global_risk_matrix.csv` (The merged output used in the dashboard).
* **Structural Clusters:** `approach1_gnn/pfas_clusters.csv` (The 7 clusters identified by the GNN).
* **Embeddings:** `approach1_gnn/embeddings_pfas.npy` (The latent vector representations of all molecules).
* **Dashboard Data:** Copies of the above stored in `platform_dashboard/data/` for the Streamlit app.
